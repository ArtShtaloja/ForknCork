Our Story

Born from a love of great food and good company, Fork n Cork has been serving bold flavors since day one. Located in the heart of Gjakovë, we believe every meal should be an experience — from the first bite to the last.

Our kitchen uses only the freshest locally-sourced ingredients, and every dish is prepared to order. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch, treating the family, or ordering for delivery — we've got you covered.
